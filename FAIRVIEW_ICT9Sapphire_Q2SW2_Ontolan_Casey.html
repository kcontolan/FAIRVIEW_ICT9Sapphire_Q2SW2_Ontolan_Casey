CAT BREEDS

Image Breed Description
Ragdoll Ragdoll cats have a reputation for being kind, serene, and friendly. Ragdolls are affectionate without being pushy, and they are almost dog-like in their devotion to their owners. Some claim that another quality that makes them the ideal companion animal is their sensitivity to emotional needs.
Persian A long-haired breed of cat with a round face and short muzzle is called a Persian cat, sometimes known as a Persian longhair. Around 1620, Persian cats were brought from Persia to Italy for the first time.
Siberian The Siberian is a centuries-old landrace of domestic cat in Russia and recently developed as a formal breed with standards promulgated the world over since the late 1980s. Siberians vary from medium to medium-large in size.
